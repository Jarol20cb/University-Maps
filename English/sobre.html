Who we are?

We are a group of students of Software Engineering and Information Systems Engineering at the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences (UPC). "University Maps" was born from the solution that we responded as a group to the problem that we raised, which is: the hard time that new students and teachers have when it is their first day of classes.

Our purpose

We want to provide a solution to a problem that is very frequent for new students and teachers who do not know the facilities of their study center
